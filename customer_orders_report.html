5. Purchase Behavior Insights

Pattern 1: Revenue concentration in high-value buyers

What we see: High-value buyers contribute 68.8% of total revenue.

Retailer action: Run VIP retention programs (early access drops, premium bundles, loyalty tiers) to protect top-revenue customers.

Pattern 2: Cross-category shopping increases spend

What we see: Customers active across categories are high spenders, with 25 customers buying in all categories.

Retailer action: Trigger cross-sell recommendations after first purchase and create category-combo offers to increase basket value.

Pattern 3: Segment-category behavior is uneven

What we see: Category spend distribution differs by customer segment, and Electronics contributes 86.7% of revenue.

Retailer action: Personalize campaign mechanics by segment: onboarding offers for low-value buyers, basket-building incentives for moderate buyers, and premium upsells for high-value buyers.

These patterns and actions are generated directly from current notebook data objects, helping convert spend behavior into targeted retail strategy.
